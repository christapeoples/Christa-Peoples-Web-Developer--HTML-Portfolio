About Me

Hello! My name is Christa Peoples, the founder of OmHustle — a lifestyle brand that blends mindfulness, ambition, and creativity. I’m an office professional, a single mother, and an entrepreneur building my own path to financial freedom through digital business and online income streams.

Guided by a Buddhist mindset, I believe success starts with clarity, consistency, and inner peace. OmHustle represents that balance — the “Om” for mindfulness, and the “Hustle” for ambition. My mission is to help others tap into both sides: the calm and the grind.

Through OmHustle, I aim to share inspiration, digital resources, and creative products that motivate people to live intentionally, work smarter, and stay grounded while building their dream life. I also offer workshops and online courses that delve deeper into personal development, helping individuals cultivate their unique strengths and navigate the challenges of modern life.

As a web developer, I understand the importance of creating an online presence that reflects one's values and aspirations. I incorporate modern web development practices to ensure that my resources are accessible and engaging for everyone.

As I continue this journey, I am committed to fostering a community where like-minded individuals can connect, share their stories, and support one another. Together, we can create a ripple effect of positivity and empowerment, encouraging everyone to pursue their passions and achieve their goals.

Join me on this exciting adventure with OmHustle, where we embrace both the tranquility of mindfulness and the drive of ambition, paving the way for a fulfilling and balanced life. Let's inspire each other to reach new heights while staying true to ourselves.
