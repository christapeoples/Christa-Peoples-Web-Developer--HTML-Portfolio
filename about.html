About Me

Hello! My name is Christa Peoples, the founder of OmHustle — a lifestyle brand that blends mindfulness, ambition, and creativity. I’m an office professional, a single mother, and an entrepreneur building my own path to financial freedom through digital business and online income streams.

Guided by a Buddhist mindset, I believe success starts with clarity, consistency, and inner peace. OmHustle represents that balance — the “Om” for mindfulness, and the “Hustle” for ambition. My mission is to help others tap into both sides: the calm and the grind.

Through OmHustle, I aim to share inspiration, digital resources, and creative products that motivate people to live intentionally, work smarter, and stay grounded while building their dream life.
